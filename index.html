About the project

This site aggregates Toronto tennis court data into a single, shareable map so players can find courts with ease, and see when public hours are available without hunting through dozens of pages. It focuses on: ease of discovery, transparency about access, and quick links to City resources and club websites. It also allows you to learn more about new clubs to play at and sharable links allow for sharing courts with others.
